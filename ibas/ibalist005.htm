Cameia National Park is situated in Moxico province in eastern Angola near the border with Zambia. It protects an area of floodplain grasslands. Dean (2001) summarised what is known about the area and listed 144 species. No new data is available.

References

Dean WRJ. 2001. Angola. Pages 71–91 in Fishpool LDC and Evans MI (eds) Important bird areas in Africa and associated islands: priority sites for conservation. Newbury and Cambridge, UK: Pisces Publications and BirdLife International.
