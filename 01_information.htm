HOME PAGE

Information is the basis for all reasoned decision making and guides careful planning and project execution. In these pages you will find the following information:

> An ornithological bibliography for Angola.

> A biennually-updated checklist of all the birds of Angola, with both English and Portuguese names, information on distribution, status and abundance and with 50 endemics and specials highlighted for special attention: The Checklist of the Birds of Angola.

> An annotated Red List of Angolan birds.

> A page of downloads of various items related to our Angolan publications.

> An annoted list of the Important Bird Areas of Angola (in progress).

> Information on birding resources for Angola, including our "The Birds of Angola" book and "Vocalisations of Angolan Birds" CD.

> Information on some key birding sites of Angola.

> Our annual Angola Research and Conservation Reports.

> A showcase of the National Bird of Angola.

> A Google Group chat line for birders called Angola Birders
